Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-18 Owner: LPagesAppLabs / D.Badi Contact: contact@lpagesapplabs.com

Summary: Clip Trimmer is a local CLI tool. It processes your video files on your machine. By default, no data leaves your device and nothing is sent to LPagesAppLabs. The only optional external call is a transcription API you configure yourself.

01 Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the clip-trimmer.lpagesapplabs.com marketing website and the Clip Trimmer CLI application.

02 Marketing Website Data

The clip-trimmer.lpagesapplabs.com marketing website:

  • Sets no cookies
  • Runs no analytics scripts
  • Stores no user data on our servers

The "Notify Me" form opens your email client locally — no server submission.

03 CLI Application: Data Processed

Clip Trimmer processes files entirely on your local machine:

  • Input video files: Read from your local filesystem, processed in-memory, written back to your filesystem. Not transmitted anywhere.
  • Transcription (Whisper): By default, Whisper runs locally on your machine with no network calls. If you configure an external transcription API (e.g. OpenAI Whisper API), audio data is sent to that provider under your account, governed by their privacy policy. This is explicitly configured by you using your own API key.
  • Face detection (OpenCV): Runs entirely on-device. No images are transmitted.
  • Output files: Written to your local filesystem as specified by you.

Clip Trimmer does not phone home, collect usage metrics, or transmit any data to LPagesAppLabs servers.

04 Third-Party Dependencies

Clip Trimmer uses open-source libraries (Whisper, OpenCV, FFmpeg, WebRTC VAD, pytest, ruff). Their use is governed by their respective open-source licenses. None of these libraries send data to LPagesAppLabs.

If you optionally configure an external API for transcription, your use of that API is subject to the privacy policy of that API provider. We recommend reviewing those policies before use.

05 Your Content

Your video files and their content are yours. Clip Trimmer does not claim any rights to content you process. We do not receive, store, or have access to your videos.

06 Your Rights (GDPR)

Given that Clip Trimmer does not collect personal data from you, there is very limited data for us to access, rectify, or delete. For any residual data (email correspondence), email contact@lpagesapplabs.com.

07 Changes to This Policy

If the tool adds network features in a future version, this policy will be updated before that version is released.
